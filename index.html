🏏 About Us

😎 Who are we?

We’re just a bunch of IPL fanatics who got tired of screaming “I KNEW IT!” at the TV… with zero proof.
So we built this app 😏

Now you can predict match winners, earn points, and finally show your friends that your cricket “gyaan” is actually elite.

No cash, no stress — just pure cricket vibes and leaderboard domination.

🎯 How it works (aka rules but make it fun)

  • Every day, a fresh IPL match pops up.
  • You pick the team you think will win. (Trust your gut… or your WhatsApp group predictions 😄)
  • Once the match ends, we update:
    • 🟢 Your points
    • 🏆 The leaderboard (where legends rise and excuses begin)

Simple. Predict → Watch → Flex.

⚠️ The Important Stuff (don’t skip this 👇)

🚫 THIS IS NOT A MONEY-BASED GAME 🚫
Read that again. No deposits. No withdrawals. No “bhai paisa double ho gaya?” here.

🧠 THIS IS A GAME OF SKILL, NOT CHANCE 🧠
Your cricket knowledge = your weapon.
Stats, form, pitch, luck (okay maybe a little 😜)… it’s all on you.

🏆 Final Word

This app is all about fun, friendly competition, and proving you’re the ultimate IPL mastermind.

So go ahead… make your predictions, climb the leaderboard, and enjoy the game like never before 💥
